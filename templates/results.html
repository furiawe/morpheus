Wordclouds

There are two types here: your standard frequency-based wordcloud, and TF-IDF wordclouds. Without going into the details, TF-IDF wordclouds show you words a particular friend uses a lot more than the rest of your friends (as opposed to regular wordclouds which just show you the words they use a lot).

% for friend in friends:
${friend['owner']} wordcloud
% endfor
